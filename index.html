Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GARP services

Is GARP only for Q1/Q2 journals or do you support all quartiles?

We support publications across all quartiles (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4) as well as reputable conferences. We believe every researcher deserves quality guidance regardless of their target publication tier.

How does the free mentorship program work?

Our free mentorship program is designed for early-stage researchers and students. You'll be matched with an experienced mentor based on your field and interests. The program includes topic guidance, methodology consultation, and writing basics through WhatsApp or video calls.

What research fields and disciplines do you support?

We support all major academic disciplines including Computer Science, Engineering (all branches), Life Sciences, Medicine, Business, Management, Social Sciences, Humanities, and interdisciplinary research. Our team of 50+ mentors covers diverse specializations.

How long does it take to publish a paper with GARP's support?

Timeline varies based on your starting point and target journal. On average: Topic development (2-3 weeks), Literature review (3-4 weeks), Writing (4-6 weeks), Review cycles (2-3 weeks), Submission to acceptance (3-6 months). We help accelerate the process through structured workflows and expert feedback.

What is your success rate for journal acceptances?

We maintain a 95% acceptance rate across all publication tiers. This includes first-time acceptances and acceptances after revision. Our success comes from rigorous quality checks, proper journal matching, and professional response to reviewer comments.

How do I get started with GARP?

Simply join our WhatsApp community or message us on Facebook Messenger. Introduce yourself, share your research interests, and our team will guide you through the next steps. There's no application fee or registration cost to join the community.
